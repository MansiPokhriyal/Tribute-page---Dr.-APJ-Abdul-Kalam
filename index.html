A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

"The People's President" & Missile Man of India

Timeline of his life

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1960: Graduated in Aeronautical Engineering from Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1980: Played a major role in India's satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1998: Contributed to India’s nuclear tests at Pokhran-II.
  • 2002-2007: Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2015: Passed away while delivering a lecture at IIM Shillong.
